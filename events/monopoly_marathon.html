Monopoly Marathon If you enjoy strategic games that test your financial management skills, this event is a must for you.

Details

  • Eligibility: OPEN FOR ALL (Age Restriction 25 Years)
  • Date of competition:
    Round 1:11th March 2023. (11.00 AM to 6.00 PM)
    Round 2: 12th March 2023. (10.00 AM to 4.00 PM)
  • Venue: BHAU's I&E Cell, North Campus, COEP Tech.
  • Team Size: Individual Entry Only
  • Registration Fees: 99 Rs/-
  • Last date to register: 10th March 2023.
  • Registration Structure: Participants have to register for the competition on the Online Platform "Townscript". Further information will be made available on the registered Email ID and the Phone number of the participant.

Structure

  • Monopoly Marathon is a board game competition with 2 rounds. Rules for both rounds are the same.
  • Round 1:
    The standard game will be played on 6 different game boards simultaneously. The total participants will be distributed randomly and equally on each of the 6 game boards. (after attendance and verification)
    The 6-hour-long game (excluding breaks) will be played in 2-hour slots. Participants are not allowed to leave during the game and will be allowed to refresh during the breaks only.
    If a player is bankrupt during the game then he/she/they will be eliminated immediately. The last standing player will be ranked 1st if the game ends before 6 hours. The player with the highest net worth will be ranked 1st at the end of the 6-hour time limit.
    The first ranker on each board will be qualified for the second round.
    A trained and experienced coordinator from the organizing team will be the banker and his/her/their decision will be final and binding on all.
    The last standing player at the end of second round will be ranked 1st if the game ends before 5 hours.
  • Round 2:
    1st rankers from each of the 6 boards will be qualified for the second round.
    The shortlisted 6 players will play the board game once again on Day 2 (12th March) with the same rules and structures as round 1.
    Round 2 game will have a time limit of 5 hours instead of 6 hours.
    Therefore, If a player is bankrupt during the game then he/she/they will be eliminated immediately. The last standing player will be ranked 1st if the game ends before 5 hours. The player with the highest net worth will be ranked 1st at the end of the 5-hour time limit.
    A trained and experienced coordinator from the organizing team will be the banker and his/her/their decision will be final and binding on all.
    In case of eliminations, the sequence of bankruptcy/elimination will be noted for shortlisting the top 3 winners. In case of no eliminations, the list of participants with their total net worth in sequence will be displayed and accordingly the top 3 winners will be selected.
    The top 3 winners of the Monopoly game will be felicitated with certificates, trophies, and the cash prize in the valedictory ceremony at the Auditorium at COEP Tech.
  • Official Hasbro Monopoly Rules for the standard game will be followed: https://www.hasbro.com/common/instruct/00009.pdf
